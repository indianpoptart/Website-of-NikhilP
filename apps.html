Ridgefield Parks and Rec

This app is for the Ridgefield Parks and Recreation customers in Ridgefield, Connecticut, Parks and Rec provides all of the information a customer needs in one place. With alerts of closings, the app provides an easy way to stay updated on events taking place at the Rec center.
